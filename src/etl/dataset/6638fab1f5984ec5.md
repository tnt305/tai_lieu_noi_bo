**Article 3. Implementation **

1. This Decree comes into force from January 09,
2026.

2. Transition:

Any complaints or lawsuits against decisions to
impose penalties for securities and securities market-related violations which
have been issued or completely executed before the effective date of this
Decree shall be considered and settled in accordance with regulations of law on
penalties for securities and securities market-related violations and relevant
laws in force at the time the violation is committed.

3. Responsibility for implementation:

Ministers, heads of ministerial agencies, heads of
Governmental agencies, Chairpersons of provincial-level People’s Committees and
relevant organizations and individuals are responsible for the implementation
of this Decree.
